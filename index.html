Welcome to Winterskloof Preschool

At Winterskloof Preschool, our teaching approach is based on a strong relationship between the child, parent / guardian and teacher. Our environment and programme encourages an active roll between all three. Winterskloof Preschool is run in a caring and nurturing manner and is devoted to the intellectual, physical, emotional and social development of your child. If you are looking for an affordable preschool that provides quality early childhood development in the Winterkloof and surrounding areas, we invite you to schedule an appointment for a tour of our preschool and experience for yourself what makes our preschool so unique!

Why choose Winterskloof Preschool?

As a parent / guardian, you want nothing more than to see your child explore and master social, emotional, physical and cognitive skills. At Winterskloof Preschool we ensure that your child feels secure and cared for whilst growing and developing in our safe and stimulating environment. Your child’s self-esteem, self-awareness, self-expression, self image and independence are amongst the things that we help grow and develop. We strive to develop positive social and emotional interactions as well as cognitive abilities. We also develop your child’s deeper understanding and awareness of their environment and that of the natural and cultural world around them. Shouldn’t your child benefit from Winterskloof Preschool’s teachings.

Mission ~ Values ~ Vision

Our Mission, Values and Vision provide guidance for our programme which is intended to foster positive growth in the lives of all our children. This allows us to adopt a holistic approach towards the overall development of each child.

  • Our first priority is the safety and overall well-being of each child.
  • Our environments are always safe, nurturing and engaging.
  • All learning experiences are tailored to meet the individual child's specific needs and development.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to provide a safe and nuturing environment for all our children to develop socially, emotionally, physically and cognitively.

Our Values

Our Values are the fundemental building blocks of our school. It includes caring, dedication, empathy, honesty, integrity, nurturing and sympathy for each and every child.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to lay the required foundation and support the diverse needs of each child.

Your child’s education and welfare are our main priority

Babies / Toddlers

1 - 3 years

Babies and toddlers naturally develop mobility, curiosity and social skills. At Winterskloof Preschool, our baby and toddler programme fosters and supports this development in a safe, caring, and nurturing way both indoors and outdoors. We provide opportunities for every child to maximize their learning potential at each developmental stage whilst learning through hands-on experiences.

Preschool

3 - 6 years

Preschoolers are naturally curious. At Winterskloof Preschool we channel this curiosity to help our children to discover, EXPLORE, IMAGINE and LEARN. It is normal for schools to have teacher initiated activities, however, at Winterskloof Preschool, we guide our preschoolers to work and think independently. This helps to build their confidence whilst advancing their mathematical reasoning, scientific thought processes and language abilities. Our school environment helps our children to challenge themselves whilst enjoying the thrill of discovering new things.

School Facilities

OUTDOORS: Our playground has a roofed jungle gym which has a slide, a ladder, a cargo net, a tyre climbing ladder, monkey bars and a rope ramp. There are additional stand alone equipment such as a trampoline, a merry-go-round, a large cargo net and a climbing structure with gymnist rings and bars with more to come.

INDOORS: Our classroom is filled with age appropriate materials and equipment for your child to explore. Theme based centres, reading areas, sensory and manipulative equipment are designed to assist your child with their development.

Free Play

We at Winterskloof Preschool recognise that each child is unique and has their own way of expressing themselves. Our free play area allows your child to decide upon the plot and to select the materials that they wish to use so as to express their creativity.

We also encourage and support your child in building independence at a young age as this is beneficial later in your child’s life. Free play has other beneficial roles. It allows for the mastering and learning of problem solving skills.

Mathematics

The key to successfully teaching mathematics lies in it being hands-on and meaningful. Children must be given opportunities to learn and develop new skills.

Our teachers together with yourself play very important roles in this regard as we provide the opportunities and support that your child needs to discover and create, learn number concepts and skills, develop confidence and solve problems.

Language Studies

Language and language skills are vital for social interactions. Language development is interdependent with brain and cognitive development and is part of your child’s overall development.

At Winterskloof Preschool we acknowledge this and provide an age appropriate reading environment with age appropriate books to assist your child with their language acquisition and other developmental milestones.

Science

Children are naturally curious and it is this curiosity that allows for exploration, inquiry and wonder about the world. At Winterskloof Preschool, we cater for this by providing areas and activities for your child to experiment and explore various scientific concepts. In doing this your child is able to practice and learn the necessary skills.

One of the benefits of teaching science in early childhood is that It gives your child the necessary grounding in scientific concepts and scientific thinking. Another benefit is that it supports the development of other skills and attributes such as communication skills, collaborative skills, team working and perseverance. It further helps your child with their analytical, reasoning and problem-solving skills.
